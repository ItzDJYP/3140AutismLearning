Understanding Autism

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition that affects how people communicate, interact, and experience the world. Every person with autism is unique, and their abilities and challenges vary.

Ways to Support Individuals with Autism:

  • Be patient and understanding.
  • Use clear, simple language.
  • Encourage routine and structure.
  • Allow extra processing time.
  • Celebrate their strengths and interests.
